### The Most Surprising Thing He Said

He assumed the entire product — including the audit logic, the savings calculations, and the plan recommendations was generated by an AI model in real time. When I told him the audit engine is actually hardcoded TypeScript rules based on verified pricing data, and that no AI touches the recommendation logic.
