## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

---

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no account, no trial period. You fill in your tools, run the audit, and see your results immediately. 

---

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**

Every pricing number in the audit engine traces back to an official vendor pricing page, verified the week of submission. The recommendations are built on fixed rules not AI guesswork so the logic is transparent and consistent.

---

**Q: Do I need to connect any accounts or share login details?**

No. Velqen never asks for account access, API keys, or integrations. You manually enter what your team pays. This means the audit runs in minutes without any security concerns, and your vendor relationships stay entirely private.

---

**Q: What if my audit shows no savings?**

We'll tell you honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid audit result and we won't manufacture savings that aren't there. If your stack is genuinely optimised, you'll see that confirmed with a breakdown of why each tool is a reasonable fit. 

---

**Q: What happens after I submit my email?**

You get a confirmation email with your savings summary and a shareable link to your audit. The shareable version strips your email and company name — only the tool breakdown and savings numbers are visible publicly.
